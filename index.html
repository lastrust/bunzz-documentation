Introduction

Introduction

Welcome to Bunzz!
Bunzz is a no-code tool for building smart contracts and DApps.

Bunzz provide various functions.

  • You can build various smart contracts by picking up prepared smart contract modules and making combinations from them.
  • You can deploy the created contracts via GUI (browser & metamask).
  • You can connect your frontend apps easily with contracts you deploy via SDK.
  • [Coming Soon! ] You can also build frontend apps via Bubble and connect to contracts with "bunzz plugin" in Bubble.
  • [Coming Soon! ] You can upload your original smart contract modules and make a combination with prepared modules.
  • [Coming Soon! ] You can monitor smart contracts you deployed and get metrics.

Let's start a journey to the web3 world with Bunzz!
